About the XVI Club

The XVI Club is the alumni association of the Cambridge University Lacrosse Club. We organise alumnin events and provide advice and financial support to CULC.

The society is named after a now-defunct group for senior players within the lacrosse club which had 16 rules.

What We Do

Varsity Match

Join us every year for the annual Varsity Match against Oxford in February.

Alumni Match

Each May we roll back the clock and take on the current students in a friendly game.

Annual Dinner

After the Alumni Match we host our annual black-tie dinner in Cambridge.
